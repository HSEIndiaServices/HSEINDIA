PROCESS AUDIT

ABOUT THE AUDIT:

A process audit is a systematic and objective examination of an organization's processes, procedures, and practices to assess their effectiveness, efficiency, and compliance with established standards. The primary goal of a process audit is to identify areas for improvement, ensure consistency, and enhance the overall quality of products or services produced by the organization.
Process audits can be applied to various aspects of an organization, including manufacturing processes, service delivery, administrative procedures, and more. They help organizations identify deviations from established processes, mitigate risks, and ensure that operations are aligned with objectives.

    AUDIT CONTENTS

  • Process Documentation.
  • Process Mapping.
  • Process Performance.
  • Compliance with Standards.
  • Efficiency and Effectiveness.
  • Process Controls and Checks.
  • Employee Understanding and Training.
  • Data Collection and Analysis.
  • Root Cause Analysis.
  • Audit Report and Recommendations.
