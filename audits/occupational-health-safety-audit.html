OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH & SAFETY AUDIT - ISO 45001:2018

ABOUT THE AUDIT:

Health and safety is a key in the workplace is promoting safe work conditions and compliance with the regulations. However, flaunting the norms or unaware of the potential hazard yield in workplace accidents. Outcome post-accident investigation reflects that the organization has not carried out occupational health and safety audit, which is considered a tool of controlling hazard. The objective of the audit is to assess compliance with established guidelines.

    AUDIT CONTENTS

  • Policy, risk assessment, health and safety policy, Review of policy, Identification of relevant hazards.
  • Evaluation of risks, Proper display of emergency contacts.
  • dequacy of natural light, Well illumination of workplace.
  • Illumination of staircases and other areas, Provision of emergency lighting.
  • Illumination survey, Welfare facilities, Housekeeping and waste management, Workstation ergonomics.
  • Floors and aisles, Special work procedures, Mechanical and heat hazards.
  • Electrical, Fire, Chemicals (general), Flammable liquids, Biological hazards.
  • Emergency equipment, Egress and evacuation, Plant technical.
  • Site technical, Compliance, Management safety, Incident/accident prevention, Medical and first aid.
  • Material storage, PPE, Slip, trip, and fall, Signage, Ventilation, Illumination etc.
