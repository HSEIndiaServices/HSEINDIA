PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT (PPE) AUDIT

ABOUT THE AUDIT:

A Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) audit is a systematic assessment of an organization's use, provision, and maintenance of personal protective equipment to ensure that employees are adequately protected from workplace hazards. PPE includes gear such as helmets, gloves, goggles, respirators, and other specialized clothing or equipment designed to minimize the risk of injuries or exposure to harmful substances. The goal of a PPE audit is to assess whether the organization is providing appropriate PPE, ensuring proper usage, and maintaining the equipment effectively.
PPE audits are crucial for maintaining a safe work environment and ensuring that employees are adequately protected from potential workplace hazards. These audits help organizations identify gaps in PPE provision, address training needs, and ensure that PPE is used correctly.

    AUDIT CONTENTS

  • Inventory and Identification.
  • Applicability Assessment.
  • PPE Selection and Fit.
  • PPE Availability.
  • Training and Education.
  • PPE Maintenance and Inspection.
  • PPE Compliance.
  • Documentation and Records.
  • Review of Incidents.
  • Documentation and Record-Keeping.
  • Audit Report and Recommendations.
