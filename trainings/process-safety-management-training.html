PROCESS SAFETY MANAGEMENT (PSM) TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Process Safety Management (PSM) training is designed to prevent and mitigate the risks associated with the handling of hazardous chemicals and processes in industries such as chemical manufacturing, petrochemicals, and refining. PSM training aims to ensure that employees understand and follows safety protocols to prevent accidents and incidents. Here are the typical contents of a Process Safety Management training program.

    TRAINING CONTENTS

  • Introduction to Process Safety Management.
  • Regulatory Framework.
  • Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment.
  • Process Safety Information.
  • Operating Procedures.
  • Safe Work Practices.
  • Employee Training and Competency.
  • Emergency Planning and Response.
  • Mechanical Integrity.
  • Pre-Startup Safety Reviews (PSSRs).
  • Management of Change (MOC).
  • Incident Investigation and Analysis.
  • Audit and Compliance Assurance.
  • Contractor Management.
  • Process Safety Culture.
  • Documentation and Record Keeping.
  • Continuous Improvement.
  • Effective Communication.
