CHEMICAL SAFETY TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

As chemical process technology becomes more complex, chemical engineers will need a more detailed and fundamental understanding of safety. The training focuses on understanding the important technical fundamentals of chemical process safety. Chemical Safety is undertaking all activities involving chemicals in such a way as to ensure the safety of human health and the environment. This Chemical Safety Training will provide knowledge about the chemicals involved and the associated work activities. Upon completing the course, participants will be able to manage chemical safety in the workplace. In addition, attendees will know the possible hazards while working in a chemical plant and the necessary precautions to be addressed. Chemical Safety training helps organisations prevent injury/fatality, achieve their obligations, and fulfil legal requirements and ISO 45001:2018.

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to Chemical Safety.
  • Hazard Communication Standard.
  • Chemical Hazards and Classes.
  • Chemical Labels and Safety Data Sheets (SDS).
  • Risk Assessment and Control Measures.
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).
  • Chemical Handling and Storage.
  • Emergency Response Procedures.
  • Ventilation and Engineering Controls.
  • Waste Disposal and Recycling.
  • Chemical Transport and Transportation.
  • Chemical Inventory Management.
  • Spill Response and Cleanup.
  • Training for Specific Chemicals.
  • Medical Surveillance.
