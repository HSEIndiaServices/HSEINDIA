HAZARD IDENTIFICATION & RISK ASSESSMENT

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

HIRA stands for Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment. HIRA training is essential for individuals and organizations to systematically identify potential hazards, assess associated risks, and implement control measures to mitigate those risks. Risk Management is a mechanism for dealing with various aspects of associated risks in managing any business activity. It is a structured approach to managing a risk related to a threat, a sequence of human activities including risk identification, risk quantification, development, and implementation of risk response/mitigation of risk using managerial resources. One of the most challenging problems for any safety representative is identifying safety and health hazards at work. Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment training program helps an organisation achieve its obligations to fulfil legal requirements and ISO 45001:2018.

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to HIRA.
  • Legal and Regulatory Framework.
  • Benefits of HIRA.
  • Hazard Identification.
  • Risk Assessment Process.
  • Risk Estimation.
  • Risk Evaluation.
  • Control Measures.
  • Hierarchy of Controls.
  • Implementing Control Measures.
  • Emergency Preparedness.
  • Monitoring and Review.
  • Documentation and Recordkeeping.
  • Integration with Management Systems.
  • Assessment and Certification.
