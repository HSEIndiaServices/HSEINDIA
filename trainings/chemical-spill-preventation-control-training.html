CHEMICAL SPILL PREVENTION TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Chemical spill prevention training is essential to educate employees and personnel about the potential hazards associated with handling and storing chemicals, as well as proper procedures to prevent chemical spills and effectively respond in case of a spill. ISO 14001 provides a framework for organizations to establish an EMS. ISO 14001 emphasizes spill prevention, control, and preparedness as part of environmental management.
<

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to Chemical Spill Prevention.
  • Chemical Hazard Identification.
  • Regulatory Requirements.
  • Chemical Storage and Handling Practices.
  • Use of Engineering Controls.
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).
  • Spill Prevention Strategies.
  • Emergency Preparedness.
  • Safe Work Practices.
  • Proper Labelling and Signage.
  • Housekeeping and Maintenance.
  • Safe Equipment Operation.
  • Communication and Reporting.
  • Hands-On Exercises.
  • Spill Response Procedures.
  • Roles and Responsibilities.
  • Case Studies and Real-Life Examples.
  • Assessment and Certification.
