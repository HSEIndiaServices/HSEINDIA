EOT CRANE SAEFTY TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

EOT Training," which typically stands for Electric Overhead Traveling Crane Training. Electric overhead traveling cranes, also known as EOT cranes, are commonly used in industrial settings for lifting and moving heavy loads. Proper training of the crane operator who is the ultimate user of the crane, goes a long way in ensuring its safe and reliable operations. Our specially designed crane operator training programmes enable operators to make optimal use of your cranes, ensure safe operations and perform periodic audio & visual checks to identify areas that require attention. Beyond training your end users, Crane edge can help you with performance planning, development of crane KPIs and performance reporting. Our crane operator training are also designed to help you with performance planning, development of Crane KPIs and performance reporting for your cranes. We believe, measuring Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for cranes helps the business to maintain high levels of productivity and operational efficiency of the crane.

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to EOT Cranes.
  • Types of EOT Cranes.
  • Components of EOT Cranes.
  • Regulations and Standards.
  • Roles and Responsibilities.
  • Risk Assessment and Pre-Work Planning.
  • Hoisting and Rigging Practices.
  • Crane Inspections.
  • Emergency Procedures.
  • Electrical Safety.
  • Communication and Signals.
  • Maintenance and Lubrication.
  • Limit Switches and Safety Devices.
  • Operational Challenges and Solutions.
