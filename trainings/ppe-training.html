PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) training is essential to educate workers on the proper selection, use, care, and maintenance of PPE to minimize the risk of workplace injuries and exposure to hazards. Personal protective equipment, commonly referred to as "PPE", is equipment worn to minimize exposure to hazards that cause serious workplace injuries and illnesses. These injuries and illnesses may result from contact with chemical, radiological, physical, electrical, mechanical, or other workplace hazards. Personal protective equipment may include items such as gloves, safety glasses and shoes, earplugs or muffs, hard hats, respirators, or coveralls, vests and full body suits. PPE training helps persons prevent injury/fatality, achieve their obligations, and fulfil legal requirements and ISO 45001:2018.

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to PPE.
  • Regulations and Standards.
  • Types of PPE.
  • Hazard Assessment.
  • Selection of PPE.
  • Proper Use of PPE.
  • Fit Testing and Seal Checks.
  • Limitations of PPE.
  • Maintenance and Care.
  • Emergency Procedures.
  • Removal and Disposal.
  • Training and Familiarization.
  • Supervision and Compliance.
  • Documentation and Records.
