BEHAVIOUR BASED SAFETY (BBS) TRAINING

ABOUT The TRAINING:

Behaviour-based safety (BBS) training focuses on understanding and improving individual Behaviour to create a safer work environment. It aims to identify and address unsafe Behaviour that can lead to accidents and incidents. Here are the typical contents of a behaviour-based safety training program.

    TRAINING CONTENTS

  • Introduction to Behaviour-Based Safety.
  • Safety Culture and Leadership.
  • Behaviour Observation and Feedback.
  • Human Factors in Safety.
  • ABCs of Behaviour.
  • Positive Reinforcement.
  • Corrective Actions and Coaching.
  • Setting SMART Goals.
  • Self-Assessment and Reflection.
  • Communication Skills.
  • Peer-to-Peer Influence.
  • Risk Perception and Decision Making.
  • Barriers to Safe Behaviour.
  • Continuous Improvement.
  • Data Collection and Analysis.
  • Creating a Behaviour-Based Safety Program.
  • Real-Life Case Studies.
