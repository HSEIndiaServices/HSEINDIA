WORK AT HEIGHT & FALL PROTECTION TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Fall protection training is essential for workers who are at risk of falling from heights during their job tasks. This training helps prevent accidents and injuries by educating workers about proper fall protection equipment use, hazard recognition, and safe work practices. Working at heights training is essential to ensure the safety of individuals who perform tasks at elevated locations. This type of training equips participants with the knowledge and skills needed to prevent accidents and handle potential hazards. Here's a comprehensive outline of the contents that could be included in a working at heights training:

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to Working at Heights.
  • Understanding Fall Hazards & Types of Fall Hazards.
  • Risk Assessment and Hazard Identification.
  • Hierarchy of Controls.
  • Proper Use of Equipment.
  • Fall Protection Systems, Fall Arrest Systems.
  • Safe Work Practices.
  • Emergency Preparedness.
  • Training on Equipment.
  • Anchorage Points.
  • Scaffold Safety.
  • Ladder Safety.
  • Emergency Rescue Procedures.
  • Communication and Coordination.
  • Training on Specific Equipment.
  • Inspection and Maintenance.
  • Documentation and Recordkeeping.
  • Weather Considerations.
  • Medical Considerations.
  • Reporting and Follow-Up.
  • Resources.
  • Review and Assessment.
