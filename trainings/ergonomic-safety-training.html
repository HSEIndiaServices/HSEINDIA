ERGONOMIC SAFETY TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Ergonomic safety training is designed to educate individuals about proper work practices, posture, and equipment use to prevent musculoskeletal disorders and promote overall well-being in the workplace. Here are the key contents that should be included in ergonomic safety training.

    TRAINING CONTENTS

  • Introduction to Ergonomics.
  • Types of Musculoskeletal Disorders.
  • Regulatory Framework.
  • Ergonomic Risk Factors.
  • Workstation Setup and Layout.
  • Proper Seating and Posture.
  • Keyboard and Mouse Use.
  • Monitor Placement and Display Settings.
  • Lifting and Material Handling.
  • Repetitive Tasks and Microbreaks.
  • Using Ergonomic Equipment.
  • Workplace Design and Layout.
  • Stretching and Exercises.
  • Assessment and Identification of Ergonomic Issues.
  • Documentation and Recordkeeping.
