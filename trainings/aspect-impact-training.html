ASPECT & IMPACT TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Aspect and impact training is focused on helping organizations identify and manage their environmental aspects and impacts. This training aims to create awareness about how an organization's activities, products, and services can interact with the environment and lead to positive or negative outcomes. Environmental aspects are one of the main elements of strategic planning as well as the foundation for an ISO 14001 Environmental Management System (EMS). Understanding of the organization’s environmental aspects is essential to determine what actions are required and how stringent the controls need to be implemented in order to mitigate the impact to the environment. The measurable results from management of the aspects will contribute to the organization’s environmental performance. The third edition of the standard, ISO 14001:2015 requires an organization to consider a life cycle perspective of its products and services in its environmental aspect’s determination.
