BIODIVERSITY & ECOSYSTEM TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Biodiversity and ecosystem training in India should focus on the unique biodiversity of the country, the importance of ecosystem conservation, and the specific regulations and initiatives in place for protecting biodiversity. Here are the typical contents of a biodiversity and ecosystem training program in India, aligned with Indian standards and considerations.

    TRAINING CONTENTS

  • Introduction to Biodiversity and Ecosystems Aspects and Impacts.
  • Biodiversity Conservation Laws and Regulations in India.
  • Biodiversity Hotspots in India.
  • Endangered Species and Conservation Efforts.
  • Ecosystem Services and Benefits.
  • Threats to Biodiversity in India.
  • Invasive Species Management.
  • Conservation of Indigenous and Traditional Knowledge.
  • Sustainable Land Use and Agriculture.
  • Wetland Conservation and Management.
  • Marine and Coastal Biodiversity.
  • Biodiversity Education and Awareness.
  • Community Participation and Local Conservation Initiatives.
  • Protected Area Management.
  • Conservation of Indigenous Flora and Fauna.
  • Wildlife Trafficking and Illegal Trade.
  • Ecosystem Restoration and Rehabilitation.
  • Global and Local Partnerships for Biodiversity.
