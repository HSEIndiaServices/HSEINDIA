5's TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

The term "5S" refers to a methodology used for workplace organization and visual management. It originated in Japan and stands for five Japanese words: Seiri (Sort), Seiton (Set in Order), Seiso (Shine), Seiketsu (Standardize), and Shitsuke (Sustain). 5S is commonly implemented in various industries to improve efficiency, safety, and overall organization. If you're looking for training content related to 5S, here's a general outline of what it might include:

    TRAINING CONTENTS

  • Introduction to 5S.
  • Step 1: Sort (Seiri).
  • Step 2: Set in Order (Seiton).
  • Step 3: Shine (Seiso).
  • Step 4: Standardize (Seiketsu).
  • Step 5: Sustain (Shitsuke).
  • Case Studies and Examples.
  • Interactive Activities.
  • Measuring Success.
  • Positive Impacts and Opportunities.
  • Documentation and Recordkeeping.
