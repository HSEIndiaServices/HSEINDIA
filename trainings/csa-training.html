CONFINED SPACE TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Confined space training is essential to ensure the safety of workers who may need to enter and work in confined spaces. These spaces can be hazardous due to limited access, poor ventilation, and potential exposure to dangerous substances. Confined space accidents are a major concern in various industries due to their hazards. Confined space training outlines the skills and protocols for safe entry to confined spaces which includes hazards, risks and precautions. Confined spaces comprise of pits, tanks, pit, well, sewer, tunnel, pipe, boiler, silo, etc. Work in confined space can kill or cause injuries in any industry, ranging from those involving complex areas to simple storage. They include not only people working in the confined space, but also for the managers, supervisors and other personnel associated with confined space, who are without adequate training. This Confined Space training and personal certification program support organisations fulfil their obligations to meet the legal and Operational Control requirements of ISO 45001:2018.

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to Confined Spaces.
  • Identifying Confined Spaces.
  • Hazards in Confined Spaces.
  • Regulations and Standards.
  • Roles and Responsibilities.
  • Entry Procedures.
  • Atmospheric Monitoring.
  • Permit Systems.
  • Emergency Rescue Plans.
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).
  • Communication and Signalling.
  • Lockout-Tagout Procedures.
  • Waste and Contaminant Removal.
  • Confined Space Entry Equipment.
  • Training for Specific Tasks.
  • Documentation and Recordkeeping.
