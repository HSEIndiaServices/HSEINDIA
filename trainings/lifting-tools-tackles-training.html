LIFTING TOOLS & TACKLES TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Lifting tools and tackles training is vital to ensure the safe use of equipment for lifting and moving heavy loads. Improper use can lead to accidents, injuries, and damage to property. Lifting tools tackles is important training to Forman, crane operator, supervisor, helper. to know about which is safe method to use. This training provides good knowledge about as given below contents as per ISO 450001:2018 standard.

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to Lifting Tools and Tackles.
  • Types of Lifting Equipment.
  • Regulations and Standards.
  • Risk Assessment and Planning.
  • Load Capacity and Weight Limits.
  • Equipment Inspection.
  • Proper Use and Operation.
  • Sling and Rigging Selection.
  • Safe Lifting Practices.
  • Communication and Signals.
  • Fall Prevention and Fall Protection.
  • Environmental Factors.
  • Emergency Procedures.
  • Documentation and Recordkeeping.
