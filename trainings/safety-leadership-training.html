SAFETY LEADERSHIP TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Safety leadership training focuses on developing the skills and mindset necessary for leaders and managers to create a culture of safety within an organization. It emphasizes the role of leadership in setting the tone for safety practices and promoting a safe work environment.

    TRAINING CONTENTS

  • Introduction to Safety Leadership.
  • Safety Culture and its Elements.
  • Leading by Example.
  • Effective Communication.
  • Safety Vision and Goals.
  • Creating Accountability.
  • Employee Engagement.
  • Risk Management and Decision Making.
  • Leading Safety Meetings.
  • Recognition and Rewards.
  • Feedback and Coaching.
  • Conflict Resolution and Problem Solving.
  • Change Management.
  • Incident Investigation and Learning.
  • Continuous Improvement and Innovation.
  • Leadership Visibility and Engagement.
  • Building Relationships.
  • Measuring Safety Performance.
