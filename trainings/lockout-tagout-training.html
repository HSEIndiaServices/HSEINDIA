LOCKOUT & TAGOUT TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Lock Out Tag Out (LOTO) is a safety procedure to ensure that the Hazardous Energy Sources are "isolated" before work begins on the equipment in question. Lock Out Tag Out protects employees from physical harm or a fatal injury from any hazardous energy while servicing or performing maintenance activities. Lock Out Tag Out training supports an organisation to overcome the risk involved in electrical, mechanical, hydraulic, pneumatic, chemical, thermal, and other energy sources. Lock Out Tag Out (LOTO) training helps an organisation prevent fatality, achieve its obligations, and fulfil legal requirements and ISO 45001:2018.

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to Lockout-Tagout.
  • Applicable Regulations and Standards.
  • Types of Hazardous Energy.
  • Roles and Responsibilities.
  • Energy Control Procedures.
  • Lockout Devices and Equipment.
  • Tagout Procedures.
  • Group Lockout/Tagout.
  • Verification and Testing.
  • Release and Removal Procedures.
  • Communication and Training.
  • Authorized Personnel Training.
  • Emergency Situations.
  • Documentation and Recordkeeping.
  • Periodic Review and Updates.
