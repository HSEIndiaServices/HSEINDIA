WASTE MANAGEMENT TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Waste management training is essential to educate individuals about proper waste handling, disposal, and recycling practices. It helps ensure that waste is managed in an environmentally responsible and compliant manner. While specific training content may vary based on the industry and regulatory requirements, here are the key contents that should be included in waste management training, along with relevant standards:
<

TRAINING CONTENTS:

  • Introduction to Waste Management.
  • Regulatory Framework.
  • Waste Classification.
  • Waste Collection and Segregation.
  • Storage of Waste.
  • Hazardous Waste Management.
  • Biomedical Waste Management.
  • Waste Transportation.
  • Waste Disposal Methods.
  • E-Waste Management.
  • Waste Minimization and Reduction.
  • Legal Compliance and Reporting.
  • Emergency Response.
  • Assessment and Certification.
