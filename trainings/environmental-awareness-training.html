ENVIRONMENTAL AWARENESS TRAINING

ABOUT THE TRAINING:

Environmental awareness training in India should address the unique environmental challenges and regulations specific to the country. Here are the typical contents of an environmental awareness training program in India, aligned with Indian standards and regulations.

    TRAINING CONTENTS

  • Introduction to Environmental Awareness.
  • Indian Environmental Legislation and Regulations.
  • National and State Pollution Control Boards.
  • Air Quality and Pollution Control.
  • Water Resources and Conservation.
  • Waste Management and Regulations.
  • Biodiversity and Wildlife Conservation.
  • Renewable Energy and Sustainable Practices.
  • Climate Change and Mitigation.
  • Community Engagement and Awareness.
  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and Environmental Initiatives.
  • Eco-Friendly Practices in Various Sectors.
  • Environmental Impact Assessments (EIAs).
  • Green Initiatives and Sustainability Reporting.
  • Role of Individuals in Environmental Protection.
  • Natural Resource Management.
  • Environmental Ethics and Cultural Values.
  • Future Trends and Challenges in Indian Environmental Conservation.
